Admission Overview

Please not that filling in the registration form does not gurantee admission

Registration forms for admission to LKG for the academic year 2020-2021 will be available online www.bhavanskochikendra.com between 30/07/2019 and 02/08/2019. Admission to classes UKG onwards is subject to availability of seats only. Parents seeking for admission may submit request letter at Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, Kochi Kendra, T D Road, Ernakulam, during the first week of March. For more details please contact Kendra Office 0484-2383451, 2383452. All students will have to attend an aptitude test in all subjects to qualify for admission


Please read all instructions carefully, before filling the ”Application Form” Take the Print out of the application form. Duly filled application form along with Photostat copies of Cap ID registration form, Degree certificate, and Rs. 200/- to be submitted at the college office. Candidates can send the application form by post along with the money order of Rs. 200/-


1. The Management reserves the right to admit or refuse pupils seeking admission to the school. Similarly, the Management decides when the question of retaining a student in the class arises. 2. No child less than 3 years old on the first day of the academic year will be admitted to L.K.G. At the time of admission, the following certificates should be attached with the application form: a) Birth certificate. (once admitted NO CHANGE OF DATE OF BIRTH WILL BE ENTERTAINED.) b) Original Transfer Certificate from the school last attended. 3. Pupils seeking admission in higher classes may be tested on the syllabus of the standard below the one to which they seek admission. 4. Admission is formally completed only when the form has been filled up and submitted to the office, fee dues are paid and the form is signed by the Principal. Payment of fees, writing the entrance test or an interview does not necessarily mean that admission is granted. 5. Admission cannot be claimed as a matter of right. Any attempt to use influence, unfair means or recommendation for admission, will lead to automatic disqualification. The school does not have any agents for admission.
